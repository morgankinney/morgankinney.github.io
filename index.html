I'm a writer, reporter, and fact-checker based in New York and Chicago. My reporting interests include science, digital culture, archival work and the environment. Sometimes I dabble in web design (e.g. this humble site). But whatever I do, there's one guiding principle: Serious stories are important, but fun stories are important, too. A former colleague distilled this philosophy into a catchphrase:

Journalism is about making friends and having a good time

— Joshua Boucher (@JAABPhoto) March 22, 2016

It's a good one, huh? We should all get T-shirts.

You'll find my abbreviated portfolio below. If you'd like to chat, shoot me an email.
