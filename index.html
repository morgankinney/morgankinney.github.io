I'm a journalist who likes to get to the point. Serious stories are important, but fun stories are important, too. A pal of mine distilled this philosophy into a catchphrase:

Journalism is about making friends and having a good time

— Joshua Boucher (@JAABPhoto) March 22, 2016

It's a good one, huh?

My reporting interests include technology, science and the environment. Sometimes I even code stuff.

While I hail from the great state of Texas, I currently study journalism at Northwestern University due to its proximity to good hot dogs. I also have an adjunct major called science in human culture, which ostensibly means "the internet."
